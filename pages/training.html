The benefits of having a trained dog are nearly limitless! From the moment you get your new puppy, he will need to start learning wrong from right for you!

Benefits of Dog Training

  • Puppy classes allow you to start your bond off to a good start. Training will allow your puppy to interact with people, other puppies and new experiences in a save environment
  • Socializing your puppy at a yound age is important to allow your dog to before a well balanced adult
  • You will learn simple solutions to common problems most dogs have
  • Traning provides your dog with basic manners including polite greetings and walking nicely
  • A trained dog can be someone that your whole family can enjoy by going for walks, hikes, swim, and visiting friends and family
  • Training has been shown to be the single most important thing that keeps a dog in his or her "forever" home
  • Training a dog forms a bond between you and your dog!

Private Training vs. Group Training

Group training would be best for puppies or older dogs that are social with other dogs and people. Group training allow people to watch each other, share their experiences and help one another understand that they often are contending with similar behaviors from their puppies. The owners come to realize that they are not alone. Group classes also teach your dog to pay attention to you even when there are distractions. Private training allows the session to be customized to you and your dogs needs. This is ideal for a dog that needs a quieter environment with fewer distractions. For some dogs it will be ideal to have a private lesson before going to group lessons.
